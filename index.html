    Performance Evaluation Framework

    We've introduced a new Performance Evaluation Framework to ensure greater clarity, transparency, and consistency in assessing performance. It outlines key evaluation areas, defined expectations, and rating guidelines. Managers will provide regular feedback and support improvement plans, while employees are encouraged to proactively track performance and communicate challenges.

    Performance Review Update (Jan - Mar 2026)

    Employees are requested to complete their performance reviews for Jan–Mar 2026 via HROne, with the three-level approval process to be finalized by April 23, 2026 (EOD). The updated Performance Evaluation Framework and revised KRAs/KPIs aim to ensure clarity, alignment with organizational goals, and a streamlined evaluation process.

    Self-assessments must be completed against defined KPIs, with supporting comments.

    Final Ratings:

    • Outstanding - 85% & Above
    • Good - Between 60% to 84%
    • Need Improvement - 59% & Below

    We hope all performance reviews have been completed as per the timeline.

    Leave/WFH/Hybrid Approval Process Update

    A revised approval process has been implemented for all Leave, WFH, Hybrid, and Time-Off requests. Employees must send requests to their Reporting Manager (RM) with leave@kalpitatechnologies.com in CC to ensure proper tracking.

    Approval from the RM is mandatory, followed by updating the request in HROne. Adhering to this process will help ensure better governance and streamlined documentation.

    Temporary Work From Home Arrangement

    In response to ongoing LPG and fuel supply challenges, a temporary work arrangement has been introduced with Thursdays and Fridays designated as WFH days to support resource conservation. This structure ensures minimal disruption to project delivery while balancing operational needs. The arrangement will be reviewed periodically, and updates will be shared accordingly.

    Expert Talk Series Launch

    A new Expert Talk Series has been introduced as a weekly knowledge-sharing initiative, scheduled every Friday from 3:00 PM to 3:30 PM. This platform encourages employees to present topics of their expertise or interest, helping build confidence and enhance communication skills.

    The sessions will be coordinated by the L&D team, with schedules shared in advance. Employees are encouraged to actively participate and make the most of this learning opportunity.
